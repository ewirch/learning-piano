Rules of the gaze

Eyes live on the page, reading one chord ahead. Ears check every landing. Hands navigate by feel — glued fingers and black-key groups. If you must look down: a planned glance only — at a line or verse break, eyes only, head up. Never stop for a fumble; fix it next lap.
