---
title: Playing Chords Without Looking
---
Accompany singing with both hands while keeping your eyes on the lyrics or chord
chart. Find the keys by feel, confirm chords by touch, and run a whole progression
without looking down.

## Lessons
- [[0001-finding-home-by-feel]] — Find any white key by feel using the black-key groups, anchor notes, and each chord's tactile fingerprint.
- [[0002-chaining-the-loop]] — Run the C–G–Am–F loop blind with one hand, using common tones as stationary anchors.
- [[0003-both-hands-blind]] — Add the left-hand bass: chord and bass together, both hands, eyes closed.
- [[0004-eyes-on-the-page]] — Put it to work — play from a chord chart with your eyes on the page, changing in time.
