  parallel fifth" — detection is solid.
- Keep the B3–A4 box, his four drilled shapes, and his fingering rule as the
  raw material; novelty budget goes entirely to the selection rules.
- He reasons precisely and corrects teachers mid-lesson (twice in the prior
  workspace, both times correctly). Trust his corrections; verify, then adopt.
