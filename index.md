title: Learning Piano
---
Short, self-contained practice workspaces for learning piano. Each one is a focused
mission with its own lessons and printable reference cards.

## Workspaces
- [[playing-chords-blindly]] — Accompany pop and folk songs with both hands while your eyes stay on the page, not the keys.
- [[selecting-inversions]] — Derive correct, efficient chord inversions on the fly — no parallel fifths, hand in one zone.
- [[naming-seventh-chords]] — Read a seventh chord on the staff and name it, and write any named seventh chord from scratch.
